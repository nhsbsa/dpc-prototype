Direct Payments to Contractors (DPC)

This service allows operational staff to manage and generate payments to NHS contractors.

This service processes data from:

  • Home Oxygen Therapy (HOT)
  • Local Area Payments (LAP)
  • Capacity Improvement Programme (CIP)

Once complete, DPC generates the file needed for the payment service (Integra) to pay each contractor.
